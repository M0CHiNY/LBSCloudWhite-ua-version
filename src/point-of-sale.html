Point of Sale

The "Point of Sale" module in LBS Cloud is a tool designed to manage sales and provide efficient customer service in stores, retail outlets, and restaurants. It offers seamless integration with inventory and accounting, providing real-time statistics and consolidation across all stores without the hassle of integrating multiple applications.

Intuitive and User-Friendly Interface

The "Point of Sale" module provides an intuitive and user-friendly interface that simplifies the sales and customer service processes. You can easily search for products, create orders, process payments, and generate invoices.

Flexible Payment
Options

The module supports various payment methods, including cash, credit cards, checks, contactless payments, and more. You can easily process payments and print receipts or electronic invoices.

Integration with Inventory and Accounting

The "Point of Sale" module is fully integrated with the "Inventory" module and accounting in LBS Cloud. This allows for automatic updating of inventory during sales and provides financial reporting related to sales.
