Warehouse

The "Warehouse" module in LBS Cloud is a tool for managing warehouse processes and inventory in a company. It helps efficiently track inventory, manage supplies, perform inventory checks, and optimize resource utilization.

Inventory
Management

The "Warehouse" module allows for accurate tracking of goods in stock. You can create product records, specify quantities, serial numbers, purchase dates, and other important information. This helps prevent losses and maintain proper inventory control.

Orders and
Deliveries

The "Warehouse" module assists in managing the process of ordering and delivering goods. You can create purchase orders, track their status, control delivery schedules, and handle storage and transportation issues.

Routing and
Placement
Optimization

The module enables the optimization of product placement in the warehouse, selection of optimal routing for goods movement, and utilization of various placement strategies to ensure efficient use of space.
